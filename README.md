# ✦ Operation Toofan — Drug-Free Pledge Platform

A gamified, interactive web platform where users take the **Operation Toofan Drug-Free Pledge**, earn a personalized certificate, and become part of a growing movement against substance abuse.

**Built with React + Vite.**

---

## What It Does

Operation Toofan transforms a simple pledge into a powerful **5-stage mission**.

### 1. Identify Yourself

Enter your name to personalize your pledge certificate.

### 2. Knowledge Check

Complete 3 short multiple-choice challenges covering:

- Peer pressure awareness
- Risks of drug use
- Supporting friends in making healthy choices

### 3. Build Your Commitment

Select up to **3 personal values** that matter most to you:

- Health
- Family
- Dreams
- Education
- Freedom
- Community
- Future Goals

Watch your pledge statement generate in real time based on your selections.

### 4. Take the Operation Toofan Pledge

Confirm your commitment to living drug-free and encouraging others to do the same.

### 5. Earn Your Certificate

Receive a professionally designed certificate featuring:

- Your name
- Personalized pledge text
- Selected value badges
- Unique Pledge ID
- Operation Toofan branding
- Downloadable PDF version
